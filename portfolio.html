  • Monty Hall Problem: Winning Strategies through Monte Carlo Simulation

    • This project uses Monte Carlo simulations to analyze the Monty Hall Problem, a classic probability puzzle where contestants decide whether to switch their initial choice of door to increase their chances of winning a hidden prize. The code simulates 1,000 rounds of the game, testing both "switch" and "stay" strategies, and calculates the probability of winning with each approach.
